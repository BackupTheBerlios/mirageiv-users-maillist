Mirage 0.9.5.2 was released today
This is a bugfix release to address two bugs that existed in the
0.9.5.1 release.

Changelog
Added ukrainian translation
Bug: Screenshot did not work correctly
Bug: Fixed a bug in the use of keymodifiers

Known bug: Mirage hangs in some cases when it asks whether or not to
wrap around the imagelist. A workaround is to use yes or no for the
wrap option.

DOWNLOAD:
as usual from:
http://mirageiv.berlios.de/download.html

Mirage-1.0 is in the making but the development is moving very slowly
at the moment.
