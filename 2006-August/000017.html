One more update here.. I ended up rewriting quite a bit of the
preloading code. It now preloads both the next and previous images,
and there's a preference to enable or disable preloading. I also did
some testing and found that if I load ~2mb jpeg files at fullscreen
(1024x768), the impact of preloading both the previous and next images
was an additional 18mb of ram usage (47mb -> 65mb), which doesn't seem
too bad.
