Hello dear Mirage users!

I find this image viewer the best for the GNOME desktop, so obviously
being on the Ubuntu Translators Team (documentation, Ubuntu-specific
applications) and a freelance GNOME/GTK applications translator
(bonfire, jokosher, pspvc, transmission - to name a few), I just had
to translate Mirage to my native language - Polish.

So here it is (messages.pot taken from 0.7.3 tarball), consider it
final. If some problems occur, don't hesitate to contact me.
